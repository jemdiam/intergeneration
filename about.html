About Intergeneration

Intergeneration is a platform dedicated to promoting diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) in the workplace, with a special focus on the value of hiring seniors in the tech industry. In an age where innovation and creativity are key drivers of success, we believe that diverse teams—composed of individuals with a variety of backgrounds, experiences, and ages—can solve complex problems and create products that resonate with a broader audience.

Why Seniors Matter

Experience is one of the most valuable assets a person can bring to a team. While younger professionals may offer new perspectives and energy, seniors bring a wealth of knowledge and proven problem-solving skills that can elevate teams to new levels of success. We aim to show companies that by creating intergenerational teams, they can foster a culture of mentorship, enhance collaboration, and unlock creativity that spans generations.

Our Vision

Inspired by the movie The Intern, our vision is to challenge the stereotypes surrounding age in the tech industry and beyond. We believe that by embracing senior talent, companies can build more resilient, empathetic, and innovative teams that create better products for everyone. Our mission is to help organizations realize the benefits of intergenerational collaboration and contribute to a more inclusive future.

What We Offer

At Intergeneration, we showcase success stories, share insights from leading companies, and offer resources to help businesses implement intergenerational hiring strategies. Through case studies, infographics, and data-backed research, we demonstrate the power of bringing together people of all ages to solve problems and drive innovation. We’re committed to fostering an environment where everyone’s experience and perspective are valued.

Join us in creating a workplace where diversity isn’t just a goal, but the key to building better, more inclusive products and services.
